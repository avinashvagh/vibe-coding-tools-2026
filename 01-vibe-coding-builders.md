# 🚀 Vibe Coding Builders (2026)

> AI-powered app builders that let you describe what you want in plain English — and get a working application back. No manual coding required.

---

## What Is a Vibe Coding Builder?

A **vibe coding builder** is an AI tool that generates full applications from natural language prompts. You describe your idea, the AI writes the code, and you get a deployable app — often in minutes.

The category was coined by Andrej Karpathy in 2025 and has exploded into a **$240M+ ARR combined market**. These tools are no longer just for prototyping — teams are shipping production apps with them.
